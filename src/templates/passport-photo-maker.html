How to make a passport photo

Pick your document type (the photo is sized to the official requirement), upload a clear, front-facing photo, then drag and zoom so your head sits inside the oval guide — the guide shows the required head height. Download a single print-ready photo, or the 4×6 sheet to print multiple copies cheaply.

This tool helps you size and crop a photo to common official dimensions, but it cannot guarantee acceptance — lighting, background, expression, and head position must still meet the issuing authority's rules. Check the official requirements (linked per document type) before submitting. Your image is processed entirely in your browser and never uploaded.
