    Tips for a photo that gets accepted

    Background: stand a little away from a plain white or off-white wall so there are no shadows behind you.

    Lighting: face an even, front-on light source to avoid shadows on your face or under your chin.

    Expression and pose: look straight at the camera with a neutral expression (a natural, closed-mouth look), eyes open, and no glasses.

    Printing: download the 4×6 sheet and print it at a pharmacy or photo kiosk, then cut out a single photo to submit.

    Common passport photo questions

    What size is a US passport photo? 2×2 inches (51×51 mm), square, with the head about 50–69% of the height. This tool sizes it for you and shows a guide.

    Can I make and print one at home? Yes. Fit your photo to the official size here, download the 4×6 sheet, and print it cheaply at any pharmacy.

    What's the 4×6 sheet for? It tiles several copies of your photo onto one standard 4×6 print, so you get multiple photos for the price of one and cut them out.

    Will it definitely be accepted? The tool gets the size and crop right, but background, lighting, expression, and head position must also meet the official rules — check them before submitting.

    Is my photo private? Yes. It is processed in your browser and never uploaded.
