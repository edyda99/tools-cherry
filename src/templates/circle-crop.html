How to crop a picture into a circle

Choose an image, then drag it inside the circle to reposition and use the zoom slider (or scroll wheel) to scale it. Pick your output size and download — the corners outside the circle are transparent, so it drops cleanly onto any background. The result is a PNG, which keeps that transparency; JPEG cannot.

Your image never leaves your device — cropping is done entirely in your browser.
