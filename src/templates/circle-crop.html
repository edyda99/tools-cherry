How to crop a picture into a circle

Choose an image, then drag it inside the frame to reposition and use the zoom slider (or scroll wheel) to scale it. Pick your output size and download — the corners outside the shape are transparent, so it drops cleanly onto any background. The result is a PNG, which keeps that transparency; JPEG cannot.

Prefer a different shape? Use the Crop shape selector to switch between a circle, a square, or a rounded square (with an adjustable corner roundness) — handy for avatars and profile pictures on platforms that use rounded tiles instead of round photos.

Your image never leaves your device — cropping is done entirely in your browser.
