How to use the tip calculator

Type your bill amount and tap a tip preset — 10%, 15%, 18%, 20%, or 25% — and the tip and grand total appear instantly. Need a different rate? Enter any number in the custom field and it takes over from the presets. To split the check, set how many people are sharing it and the calculator shows what each person owes, including their share of the tip.

If you'd rather tip on the food and drink only, enter the tax that's included in your bill and switch on Tip on the pre-tax amount — the tip is then calculated on the subtotal, not the tax. The rounding options let you bump the total (or just the tip) up to the next whole dollar, which makes paying in cash or settling a group bill simpler.

How much should you tip?

In the United States the common guideline for sit-down restaurant service is 15% as a minimum, 18% for good service, and 20% or more for excellent service. Tipping customs vary by country and by the type of service, so treat these as a starting point rather than a rule. A fast way to estimate 20% in your head is to find 10% of the bill by moving the decimal one place to the left, then double it.

Nothing you enter leaves your device — every calculation runs in your browser, with no signup and nothing stored.
