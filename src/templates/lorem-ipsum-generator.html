Lorem Ipsum questions

What is Lorem Ipsum? It is scrambled, meaningless filler text derived from Latin, used as a placeholder so you can judge a layout without being distracted by real wording. It has been the standard dummy text of the printing industry since the 1500s.

Why use placeholder text? It fills a design with realistic-looking words so you can assess spacing, line length, and typography before the final copy exists — and final copy is rarely ready when layout work begins.

How much should I generate? Roughly the amount your real content will take up. A sentence or two for a blurb, a few paragraphs for an article. Switch units and adjust the count to match.

Does it always start with “Lorem ipsum”? By default yes, since that is what most people expect, but you can turn the classic opener off to start with random words instead.

Is it free and private? Yes — it is free, runs entirely in your browser, generates text locally, and uploads nothing, so it even works offline once loaded.
