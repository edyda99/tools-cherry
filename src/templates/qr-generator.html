How to use the QR code generator

Pick a type, fill in the details, and your QR code updates instantly. For a WiFi code, anyone who scans it with a phone camera can join your network without typing the password. For a contact card, scanning adds your details straight to the phone's contacts. Download as PNG for documents and printing, or SVG for crisp scaling at any size.

QR codes are generated entirely in your browser — the data you enter is never uploaded.
